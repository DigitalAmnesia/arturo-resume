About

I am a “problem-solver” focused on practical responses for “day to day” issues. Passionate about Intellectual Property and the Law. Corporate counsel and IP specialist with more than 20 years of experience.

Attorney & Brand Protection Manager.

I have had the honor of working for 3 global companies in the last 14 years. From 2017 to 2021 I worked for Underwriters Laboratories as Brand Protection Manager, dealing with piracy across the region (Latam) and providing legal assistance to the company’s Mexican office;

From 2017 to 2007 I worked at Schneider Electric as head of the program to fight counterfeits and unfair competition, and then as Senior attorney in charge of litigations, IP and corporate investigations.

In 2006 I worked also as Senior attorney at AstraZeneca coordinating the legal defense of the company’s patents on public tenders and other administrative matters.

Before becoming an in-house counsel I worked for 3 of the most prestigious legal Firms in Mexico (Oliares and Cía, Hogan Lowels and Becerril Coca & Becerril) as associate or executive counsel in the area of Intellectual Property focused on IP protection and appeals.
